Feeling Tempted Right Now?

Don't wait — use your emergency plan immediately!

⚔️ Your Battle Strategy

1

STOP Immediately

Pause everything. Put down your device. Physically step away from the situation.

2

PRAY Out Loud

"Lord Jesus, I need Your strength right now. I cannot do this alone. Fill me with Your Spirit."

3

SPEAK Scripture

Say aloud: "No temptation has overtaken me except what is common to mankind. God is faithful!" (1 Cor 10:13)

4

CHANGE Environment

Go outside. Call someone. Do pushups. Get to a different room. Physical movement breaks the mental pattern.

5

LOG It

Whether victory or struggle, log it here. This data helps you grow stronger. No shame — only growth!
